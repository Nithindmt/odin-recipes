BDF

Description

Also known as Kosher Bacon, our Beef Fry is known around the world as Kosher Bacon. No, not Kevin Bacon, the real stuff. Ok, it might be more than six degrees removed, but it's succulent, salty, meaty, fatty juiciness just oozes with flavor. It's like nothing you've ever experienced.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Sear all sides of beef
  2. Always place the roast fat side up in the roasting pan
  3. Season with herbs
  4. Do not add liquids or salt
  5. Cook in the oven, uncovered as per your recipe. For tender beef, roast at 160°C (320°F)
